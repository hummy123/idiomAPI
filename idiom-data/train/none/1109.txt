This is a shocking state of things ; and in spite of everything that the Jenkinsons and the Scots can do , this state of things must be changed . At Sarr , or a little way further back , I saw a man who had just begun to reap a field of canary seed . The plants were too far advanced to be cut in order to be bleached for the making of plat ; but I got the reaper to select me a few green stalks that grew near a bush that stood on the outside of the piece .
